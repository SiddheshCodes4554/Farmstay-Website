Meet the Owner

John Snow

John Snow, a third-generation farmer, founded Snow's Riverside Farmstay in 2010. With a passion for sustainable agriculture and a desire to share the beauty of rural life, John transformed his family farm into a welcoming retreat for visitors from all walks of life.

John's extensive knowledge of local flora, fauna, and farming practices ensures that guests not only enjoy a comfortable stay but also gain insights into the rich agricultural heritage of the region.

Our Aim
